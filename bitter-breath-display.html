Bitter Breath
Brother of Bloodbath — Medium fiend (demon), chaotic evil
"Smaller, they said. Always smaller. Let me show you what smaller means."

Armor Class 19 (natural armor)

Hit Points 280 (buffed for party of 3 at level 11)

Speed 50 ft.

Challenge 16 (15,000 XP)

Traits

Blood Frenzy. Bitter Breath has advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its hit points.

Evasive Gore. When Bitter Breath is hit by an attack, he can use his reaction to halve the damage if he can see the attacker. He then moves up to 15 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

Aura of Rot. Any creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of Bitter Breath must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of its next turn. While poisoned in this way, the creature's speed is halved.

Magic Resistance. Bitter Breath has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Bitter Breath fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Relentless (1/Day). If Bitter Breath is reduced to 0 hit points, he drops to 1 hit point instead.

Actions

Multiattack. Bitter Breath makes three Gutripper attacks, or two Gutripper attacks and one Gore Horns attack.

Gore Horns. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d10 + 7) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or Bitter Breath moves to the opposite side of the target (without provoking opportunity attacks) and makes one additional Gore Horns attack as part of the same action.

Gutripper. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8 + 7) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. On a critical hit, the target begins bleeding, taking 7 (2d6) necrotic damage at the start of each of its turns until it receives magical healing or a creature uses an action to stanch the wound with a successful DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine) check.

Rotten Breath (Recharge 5–6). Bitter Breath exhales a 40-foot cone of putrid, corrosive gas. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, taking 36 (8d8) poison damage and 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature that fails the save by 5 or more is also stunned until the end of its next turn.

Bonus Actions

Skirmisher. Bitter Breath takes the Dash or Disengage action.

Legendary Actions

Bitter Breath can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature's turn. Bitter Breath regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

Reposition. Bitter Breath moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Gutripper Strike. Bitter Breath makes one Gutripper attack.

Flaying Rush (Costs 2 Actions). Bitter Breath moves up to his speed. Each creature he passes within 5 feet of during this movement must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (4d6) slashing damage. He can make one Gutripper attack at the end of this movement.

Tactics

Bitter Breath fights nothing like his brother. Where Bloodbath stood his ground and gorged, Bitter Breath never stops moving. He opens with Rotten Breath to stun and poison the cluster, then uses Skirmisher and his 50-ft. speed to dart between targets, focusing wounded PCs with Blood Frenzy advantage. His legendary Reposition and Flaying Rush ensure he is never where the party expects. He uses Evasive Gore to punish melee attackers and reposition on reaction. Relentless means the party thinks he's down — and then he's not.

Personality & Dialogue

Cold and bitter. Speaks in short, venomous sentences. Resents his brother's fame. Doesn't roar — he hisses. Doesn't boast — he dismisses.

"He drank blood like a fool. I let it drain slowly."
