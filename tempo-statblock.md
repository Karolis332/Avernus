# TEMPO, THE DEVIL'S MAESTRO

**Medium fiend (devil), lawful evil**

*He appears as a slender, immaculately dressed man with ash-grey skin and fingers too long for his hands. His smile never falters. His violin is carved from bone and strung with sinew that hums even when he isn't playing. When he speaks, every word sounds rehearsed — because it is.*

---

**AC** 17 (infernal finery) | **HP** 195 (23d8 + 92) | **Speed** 30 ft., fly 40 ft. (hover)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|

**Saving Throws** Dex +9, Con +9, Wis +7, Cha +11

**Skills** Deception +11, Insight +7, Performance +16, Persuasion +11

**Damage Resistances** cold; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered

**Damage Immunities** fire, poison

**Condition Immunities** charmed, poisoned

**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12

**Languages** Common, Infernal, Sylvan; telepathy 120 ft.

**Challenge** 14 (11,500 XP)

---

## Traits

**Devil's Sight.** Magical darkness doesn't impede Tempo's darkvision.

**Magic Resistance.** Tempo has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Legendary Resistance (3/day).** If Tempo fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

**Maestro's Presence.** Any creature that starts its turn within 30 feet of Tempo and can hear him must succeed on a **DC 18 Wisdom save** or be charmed until the start of its next turn. While charmed this way, the creature is incapacitated and has speed 0, transfixed by the music. A creature that succeeds is immune for 24 hours. Creatures that are deafened automatically succeed.

**The Smiling Devil.** Tempo has advantage on Charisma checks against creatures that have previously signed an infernal contract or made a bargain with a fiend.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Tempo makes three Discordant Note attacks, or one Bow Strike and two Discordant Note attacks.

**Discordant Note.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +11 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d10 + 6) psychic damage. If the target is charmed, this attack deals an extra 7 (2d6) psychic damage.

**Bow Strike.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing + 10 (3d6) psychic damage. The violin bow leaves a wound that weeps black ichor.

**Cacophony (Recharge 5–6).** Tempo unleashes a devastating wave of dissonance in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a **DC 18 Constitution save**, taking 36 (8d8) thunder damage and being deafened for 1 minute on a failure, or half damage and no deafness on a success. A deafened creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns. *(Note: deafened creatures are immune to Maestro's Presence.)*

**Captivating Melody (1/day).** Tempo plays an irresistible piece. Each creature of his choice within 60 feet that can hear him must succeed on a **DC 18 Wisdom save** or be charmed for 1 minute. While charmed, the creature is compelled to move toward Tempo on each of its turns and cannot willingly attack him. A charmed creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, with advantage if it has taken damage since its last turn.

---

## Reactions

**Countermeasure.** When a creature Tempo can see within 60 feet casts a spell, Tempo plays a discordant counter-melody. The caster must succeed on a **DC 18 Charisma save** or the spell fails and has no effect (as *counterspell*). Tempo can use this reaction 3 times per day.

---

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

Tempo can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

**Discordant Note.** Tempo makes one Discordant Note attack.

**Reposition.** Tempo moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Soul Riff (Costs 2 Actions).** One creature Tempo can see within 60 feet must make a **DC 18 Charisma save**, taking 22 (4d10) psychic damage on a failure, or half on a success. If this reduces a creature to 0 HP, Tempo captures its soul in his contract ledger.

**Standing Ovation (Costs 3 Actions).** Each creature charmed by Tempo must use its reaction to make one melee attack against a target of Tempo's choice within its reach.

---

## Roleplaying Tempo

**Personality:** Never raises his voice. Never stops smiling. Treats combat like a performance — he narrates, critiques, and applauds. He is *genuinely delighted* to see Nico again.

**Voice:** Calm, melodic, every sentence paced like music. Slight pauses before the important words.

**Key Lines:**
> "Nico. My finest student. I've kept your seat warm."

> "You cheated, you know. Changed the flyers, stacked the audience. I respected that. It showed *ambition*. But ambition without talent is just noise."

> "I didn't take their souls because I'm cruel. I took them because you *asked* me to. Every prayer to your pillow — I heard every one."

> "You want a rematch? How delightful. Same terms?"

**In Combat:**
> "Flat. Try again."

> "There it is — that fire. I knew it was still in you."

> *(when bloodied)* "Now *this* is a performance worth remembering."

**If Defeated:**
> "Bravissimo... You've improved."
> *(His contract ledger falls open. The souls bound within cry out.)*
