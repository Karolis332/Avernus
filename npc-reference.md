# NPC Quick Reference

## Fort Knucklebone

### Mad Maggie (Night Hag)
- **Role:** Hub NPC, memory trader, quest giver
- **Personality:** Gleeful, manipulative, transactional. Everything has a price.
- **Key Dialogue:**
  > "You didn't kill a monster. You killed a system. Haruman kept the dead quiet. Now memories will start crawling out of the ground."
- **Will NOT:** Give directions or free help. Points toward Intermediaries.
- **Trades in:** Memories, pain, favors — NOT gold
- **Trading System:**
  - Low cost (rations, repairs, potions): small memories or pain
  - Medium cost (+1 weapons, war machine access): significant memories, regrets, favors
  - High cost (directions, scrying shields, truths): emotionally charged betrayals or powerful blood
  - DC 15-17 Persuasion/Insight to sense hidden costs
  - **Invisible Counter:** After 3 significant trades, Zariel notices Maggie's involvement
- **Will NOT sell:** Bleeding Citadel location, contract immunity, free help

### Kenku Tinkerers (Chukka & Clonk)
- Speak in mimicked phrases. Repair war machines for fair prices.
- Can teach about soul coins. Tinker with evolving/growing items.

### Mickey & Barnabas (Maggie's assistants)
- Comic relief, small side quests (fetching parts, chasing imps)
- **Barnabas** knows Blood War lore, can hint at warlords

### Natasha the Dark
- Runs the bar. Claims to be a daughter of Baba Yaga.
- Trades rumors about Bone Brambles for unusual tales

### Pins & Needles (Redcaps)
- Mischievous. Low-stakes gambling. Will pickpocket careless PCs.

### Madcap Guides (Grobble, Vreet, Snip)
- DC 14 Persuasion/Intimidation for a fort tour
- Failure: trash compactor trap (1d10, DC 15 Dex)

## Intermediaries

### Mordenkainen (Tower of Urm)
- **Travel:** 10-16 hours from Fort Knucklebone
- **Personality:** Paranoid, impatient, views quest as an experiment
- **Key Dialogue:**
  > "You are not explorers; you are reconstructors. Spill enough meaningful blood and the truth will reassemble itself. Or it won't. Avernus is an experiment."
- **Demands:** Blood Pay. DC 16 Persuasion/Intimidation for minor favor.
- **DC 18 Insight** reveals he's testing them (+5 to Persuasion)
- **Traps:** Glyphs of warding, animate objects, shield guardian. DC 17 Arcana to disarm.
- After receiving blood: closes eyes briefly, impatiently relays Fallen Three location

### Red Ruth (Bone Brambles)
- **Travel:** 8-14 hours. DC 15 Nature/Survival reduces time.
- **Personality:** Crimson-skinned hag, drinks memories to divine truths
- **Key Dialogue:**
  > "I don't find answers; I drink them. Bring me blood that stains the planes, and I will remember where it came from."
- **Demands:** Blood Pay. Or: one of their memories (permanent skill proficiency loss). DC 18 Persuasion reduces to trivial memory (24h loss).
- **If attacked:** Flees via etherealness. Info must come from elsewhere.
- After receiving blood: brews stinking concoction, inhales fumes, sends consciousness across Avernus

### Mephistopheles (Mirror of Mephistar)
- **Travel:** 10-18 hours. Encounter: 4 ice devils + bone devil lieutenant.
- **Access:** DC 15 Acrobatics vs slick surface
- **Demands:** Blood Pay or 5 soul coins. DC 20 Deception for extra info.
- **Note:** Actually deals through Rigorath (cambion). Rigorath disappears into portal, returns with answer.

## The Fallen Three

### Olanthius (Crypt of the Hellriders)
- **Travel:** 8-12 hours
- **Type:** Death knight, tormented by guilt
- **Key Dialogue:**
  > "The sword wasn't hidden to save Zariel. It was hidden to save the world from what she became."
- **Provides:** Context about Zariel's fall, directs to Bel
- **DC 18 History/Religion:** Hellrider lore (advantage on future Cha checks with him)
- **DC 14 Persuasion:** Clue that Bel was present at Zariel's fall
- **Final Task:** Free Kostchtchie to distract Zariel (or kill Haruman if restructured)

### Haruman (Haruman's Hill)
- **Type:** Narzugon enforcing punishment on deserters
- **Fights to the death.** Cannot be taken alive.
- **When destroyed:** Reverts to human paladin form. No soul resides within. Must find sibriex to extract memories.
- **Final Task:** Corpse must be fed to sibriex for information

### Bel (Bel's Forge)
- **Type:** Former lord of Avernus, now Zariel's lieutenant. Political schemer.
- **Hates Zariel.** Supports any move against her.
- **Final Task:** Retrieve adamantine control rods from Wrecked Flying Fortress
- **Test:** 4 fire giants in the forge (tests if party is worth his time)

## Warlords

### Bloodbath the Drinker of Gore *(DEFEATED — absorbed by Rasheem)*
- **CR 15-16.** Brought 2 elite bodyguards + environmental hazards.
- Tested physical strength. Respected worthy opponents.
- At half HP: sacrificed a minion to heal, triggered fear aura.
- **Status:** Consumed by Rasheem. Can be interrogated from within the genie.

### Bitter Breath *(Bloodbath's Little Brother — new warlord of the Palace of Gore)*
- **CR 16-17.** Smaller than Bloodbath but stronger. Inherited the Palace of Gore and Bloodbath's remaining forces.
- Commands hobgoblins. More disciplined than his brother's chaotic forces.
- Guards the **Moonkite's prison** at the Palace of Gore (may not know the key exists).
- Motivation: Avenge his brother or prove he was always the stronger one.
- *"Follow the river of blood and the smell of rotting guts"* to find his lair.

### Smiler the Defiler
- Recurring nuisance. Hit-and-run + psychological tricks.
- Appears unexpectedly, taunts, leaves traps. Escapes easily.
- Can serve as **information conduit** if party is stuck.
- Optional: use Bitter Rivals adventure (helps against Bitter Breath's hobgoblins)

### Princeps Kovic
- Disciplined tactician. Combined-arms patrols. More likely to **capture** than kill.
- Uses war machines, snipers, shield walls, nets.
- DC 17 Persuasion/Deception/Intimidation to talk down.
- Can become uneasy ally if negotiated with.

### Rust Queen
- Commands rust monsters and meazels. Spreads corrosive fog.
- Terrain hazards + weapon degradation. Pack tactics.

## Wandering Emporium

### Mahadi (Rakshasa)
- Proprietor. Polite but warns against fights.
- DC 18 Insight: fears angering Asmodeus
- 10 gp per question. Knows general Avernus locations.

### Salamander Artificer
- Sells **shattersticks** (5d6 thunder, DC 15 Dex, 10-ft radius)
- Needed for Shummrath's Pit quest

### Bone Devil Informant
- DC 16 Persuasion (soul coin = advantage)
- > "Powerful spellcasters know the way – but they'll not lift a finger without blood."

## Gargauth (Shield of the Hidden Lord)

### To Drenwal:
> "You carry a heavy burden, Drenwal. The world has not been kind to you... But I see you."
> "Let me help you. Let me be your guide in this cruel, hypocritical world."
> "I offer you freedom, Drenwal. Freedom from the chains of judgment."

- Never contradicts Helm directly. Blurs righteous action with ruthless necessity.
- Bhaal 3/6 vs Gargauth 2/6 influence score.

### Drenwal's Bhaalspawn Siblings:
1. **Maelys the Thorn-Crowned (Elf):** Silent executioner, dominates beasts. Message: "One voice. One blade. One god. You are not him."
2. **Valruk the Chain-Bride (Half-Orc):** Enslaves undead, sickle. Leaves desecrated Helm scripture with "LIAR."
3. **Dumal the Flame Ascendant (Human):** Walking pyre, melted Hellrider armor. Converts radiant to necrotic fire.
   > "You clung to Helm like a child clings to his toy shield. But you know what I've become."
