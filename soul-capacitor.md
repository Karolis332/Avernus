# Soul Capacitor

*Wondrous item (brass lamp), rare (evolving), requires attunement by a rogue with the Soulknife subclass*

A brass genie lamp threaded with crystalline psionic channels that pulse with faint violet light. The lamp is warm to the touch and hums at a frequency only the attuned creature can feel. It was gifted by Rasheem, guildmaster of an interplanar bounty hunter syndicate, and serves as both a weapon augment and a soul-trapping instrument. The Soul Capacitor grows in power not from wealth, but from completed bounties and collected souls.

> *"Remember — there are no friends in the guild, only situational acquaintances."*
> — Rasheem, Guildmaster and one Sexy Genie

---

## Properties

**Psionic Bond.** When you attune to the Soul Capacitor, it bonds to either your main hand or off-hand Psychic Blade. That blade gains a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.** The bond consumes one attunement slot. You may bond a second attunement slot to empower your other hand's blade as well.

**Weapon Consumption.** As part of a short or long rest, you can feed a magic weapon into the Soul Capacitor. The weapon is destroyed and its essence is partially absorbed. Add the consumed weapon's enhancement bonus to the lamp's total, then divide by half (rounded down). The result replaces the lamp's base +1 bonus, split between both hands. If only one hand is bonded, the full bonus applies to that hand. The consumed weapon is teleported to Rasheem's sanctum.

**Contracts.** The genie Rasheem provides bounty contracts through the lamp. Completing bounties evolves the Soul Capacitor, unlocking new soul tiers and burning permanent bounty soul imprints into the lamp's crystalline channels. See *Bounty Souls* and *Bounty Progression.*

---

## Soul Bounty

The lamp passively captures fragments of slain souls, storing their psionic residue for later use.

**Capture.** When a creature you can see dies within 30 feet of you, you can use your reaction to attempt to trap its soul. The soul makes a Dexterity saving throw (DC equals 10 + the creature's CR). On a failed save, the soul is stored in the lamp. On a success, the soul escapes.

**Storage.** You can hold a number of souls equal to half your proficiency bonus (rounded down). You cannot capture a soul if your storage is full.

**Consumption.** As a bonus action (combat effects) or over the course of 1 minute (roleplay effects), you can consume a stored soul to produce an effect determined by its tier. The soul is destroyed once consumed. You choose the effect when you consume the soul.

## Field Soul Tiers

### Minor Souls
*CR 1-4, Small or Medium creatures. Available at base.*

**Combat.** Gain advantage on your next attack roll with a Psychic Blade. This effect lasts until the end of your next turn.

**Roleplay.** Choose one:
- You can perfectly mimic the creature's voice or general appearance (as a faint illusionary aura) for 1 hour.
- You unlock faint memories from the soul, granting you advantage on one Intelligence (Investigation) or Charisma (Deception) check related to that creature's or associates.

---

### Medium Souls
*CR 5-10, any size. Unlocked by completing the Bloodbath bounty.*

**Combat.** Choose one:
- ***Psionic Surge.*** Your next Psychic Blade attack that hits deals an additional 2d8 psychic damage. If this attack deals Sneak Attack damage, you add one extra Sneak Attack die to the damage (this extra die doesn't count toward your normal Sneak Attack total).
- ***Soul Shiv.*** For the next minute, when a creature within 30 feet of you deals damage to you, you can use your reaction to launch a psychic shard at it. The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC equals 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier) or take 3d6 psychic damage and be frightened of you until the end of its next turn.

**Roleplay.** Choose one:
- ***Memory Siphon.*** You experience a vivid sensory flashback of the creature's final 24 hours — what it saw, heard, and feared. This functions as the *speak with dead* spell, limited to events within that timeframe. This takes 1 minute.
- ***Soul Mask.*** You perfectly replicate the creature's appearance, voice, and mannerisms for up to 4 hours. This is a physical transformation (not illusory — it is tactile and passes casual inspection). For the duration, you gain proficiency in one skill the creature possessed.
- ***Psionic Echo.*** For 1 hour, you can sense creatures of the same creature type within 60 feet of you. You know their number and general direction but not their exact location.

---

### Major Souls
*CR 11-16, any size. Unlocked by completing a future bounty contract.*

If the creature had Legendary Resistance and used it before dying, the capture DC is reduced by 2 for each use.

**Combat.** Choose one:
- ***Psychic Detonation.*** As an action, you release the soul as a psychic explosion. Each creature of your choice within 20 feet of you must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC equals 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier). On a failed save, a creature takes 6d8 psychic damage and is stunned until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn't stunned. You can still use your bonus action to make an off-hand Psychic Blade attack on this turn.
- ***Soul Predator.*** For 1 minute (concentration), your Psychic Blades shimmer with captured essence. Your Psychic Blade attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20, and your critical hits with Psychic Blades deal an additional 3d8 psychic damage. For the duration, you can see invisible creatures within 30 feet of you.
- ***Vengeful Shade.*** You can use this as a reaction when you are reduced to 0 hit points. The consumed soul erupts from the lamp and manifests as a spectral copy of you. The shade uses your stat block, has temporary hit points equal to the consumed soul's CR multiplied by 5, acts on your initiative count, and can make your Psychic Blade attacks. It lasts for 3 rounds or until its temporary hit points are depleted. You continue making death saving throws while the shade fights.

**Roleplay.** Choose one:
- ***Deep Memory Extraction.*** Over the course of 10 minutes, you experience key moments from the creature's entire existence — alliances, enemies, secrets, and one piece of information it would never willingly reveal. For the next 24 hours, you have advantage on all Intelligence and Charisma checks related to that creature and its known associates.
- ***Soul Inhabit.*** You assume the creature's full physical form for up to 8 hours (as *alter self*, but a complete transformation including size, features, voice, and scent). You gain one of the creature's non-magical, non-legendary abilities (DM's discretion). Creatures familiar with the original must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom (Insight) check to notice something is wrong.
- ***Bounty Network Ping.*** Rasheem traces the soul's connections through the syndicate network. You learn the names and general locations of 1d3 creatures the consumed soul had significant dealings with.

---

### Legendary Souls
*CR 17+, any size. Unlocked by completing an extraordinary bounty.*

Legendary Souls occupy 2 storage slots. You can hold only one Legendary Soul at a time. Creatures with Mythic traits impose disadvantage on the capture Dexterity saving throw.

**Combat.** Choose one:
- ***Annihilation Pulse.*** As an action, you channel the legendary soul's full power through your blade. Make a single melee attack with your Psychic Blade. On a hit, the attack deals its normal damage plus your Sneak Attack damage (if applicable) plus 10d10 psychic damage. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC equals 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier) or be paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. On a miss, the target takes half the psychic damage and is not paralyzed. After you use this ability, the Soul Capacitor goes dormant — you cannot capture or consume souls until you finish a long rest.
- ***Gestalt Form.*** For 1 minute (concentration), the legendary soul merges with your consciousness. For the duration, your Psychic Blade attacks deal an extra 2d10 psychic damage on each hit, you have resistance to all damage, your speed increases by 20 feet, and you gain truesight out to 60 feet. When the effect ends, you gain 2 levels of exhaustion.
- ***Soulcatcher's Gambit.*** When a creature within 60 feet of you dies, you can use your reaction to attempt to capture its soul (normal Dexterity saving throw applies) even if you didn't deal the killing blow. For the next hour, you can release the captured soul as a bonus action. It manifests as a translucent **Phantom Ally** with half its original hit points, its basic attacks, and acts on your initiative count. It obeys your mental commands (no action required). It cannot use legendary actions, lair actions, or spellcasting above 3rd level.

**Roleplay.** Choose one:
- ***Soul Archive.*** The legendary soul's complete history is permanently imprinted in the lamp's crystal channels. You can consult it as a living library, asking questions and receiving answers as if you had cast *legend lore* with perfect accuracy about anything the creature knew. This persists even after the combat effect is consumed. You can make 3 consultations per long rest.
- ***Legendary Guise.*** You assume the creature's form for up to 24 hours. You gain two of the creature's non-legendary abilities. Creatures who knew the original in life have disadvantage on Wisdom (Insight) checks to see through the disguise. You can speak any language it knew.
- ***Rasheem's Favor.*** Completing a legendary bounty earns significant standing in the syndicate. Rasheem provides a one-time boon appropriate to the bounty (a rare item, a contact on another plane, a safe house, or intelligence on a future target).

## Bounty Souls

When Rasheem processes a completed bounty, the syndicate claims the bulk of the target's essence. But a residual imprint — a psychic scar — remains burned into the lamp's crystalline channels. These **Bounty Soul imprints** are permanent. They do not occupy storage slots. Their abilities recharge as noted.

Each Bounty Soul carries a **corruption risk** — channeling the essence of powerful, often evil creatures leaves a mark on the wielder.

---

### Bounty Soul #1: Bloodbath, Drinker of Gore
*Imprinted after Bounty #2. The capacitor occasionally weeps red when this imprint is active.*

**Bloodscent (Passive).** You can smell fresh blood within 60 feet of you. You know when any creature within 60 feet of you is below half its hit point maximum (you perceive this as a faint red pulse through the lamp's surface).

**Drinker's Frenzy (1/Long Rest).** As a bonus action, you channel Bloodbath's predatory instinct. For 1 minute, the following effects apply:
- When you hit a creature that is below its hit point maximum with a Psychic Blade attack, the attack deals an extra 1d8 necrotic damage. The blade visibly drips red.
- When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points, you regain hit points equal to your rogue level.
- Your eyes glow crimson and your Psychic Blades take on a blood-red hue.

***Corruption — Blood Hunger.*** When the effect ends, you must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be compelled to use your next turn to move toward and attack the nearest creature that is below its hit point maximum (ally or enemy). The DC starts at 13 and increases by 1 each time you use Drinker's Frenzy (cumulative across days). The DC resets to 13 after you go 3 consecutive days without using this ability.

**Bloodbath's Memories (1/Long Rest).** You can spend 1 minute meditating to commune with Bloodbath's residual imprint. The memories are fragmented and violent — flashes of the Palace of Gore, faces of enemies, territorial knowledge. You can learn:
- Warlord politics in Avernus (alliances, rivalries, betrayals)
- The Palace of Gore's layout (advantage on Intelligence checks to navigate or investigate within or near the Palace)
- Names and descriptions of devils, demon lords, and smugglers Bloodbath dealt with
