Fort Knucklebone
Neutral ground • War machine repairs • Trading post
What You Can Do Here
  • Repair war machine (1-2h)
  • Trade with Mad Maggie
  • Visit the Well (potable water)
  • Gamble at the Arcade
  • Buy/sell with soul coins

▶
A ramshackle fortress of bone and scrap metal rises from the blasted hellscape, its walls patched with infernal iron and dragon hide. Kenku tinkerers chatter and hammer at war machines in the yard, while the acrid smell of brimstone mingles with the improbable scent of cooking food.
Features

Kenku tinkerers, the Well (potable water — rare in Avernus), soul coin economy. Neutral ground enforced by Mad Maggie.

Key NPCs

Mad Maggie — Night hag merchant. Trades in soul coins and favors. Knows the layout of Avernus. Shrewd but fair (by hag standards).

Kenku Tinkerers — Repair war machines. Communicate through mimicry.

What You Can Do Here
  • Seek intel on Blood Pay locations

▶
A slender tower of dark stone materializes from the swirling ash like a mirage given form. Its walls shimmer with barely-contained arcane energy, and the air crackles with the residue of a hundred wards. This is no permanent fixture of Avernus — it phases in and out of the plane at Mordenkainen's whim.
Hazards & Encounters

Spell-trapped halls: Glyphs of warding (lightning, confusion), animate objects, shield guardian. DC 17 Arcana to disarm each.

Key NPC

Mordenkainen (Simulacrum) — Impatient, condescending, experimental. Not the real archmage — a simulacrum left to observe Avernus. Will share information if impressed or persuaded. DC 16 Persuasion for a minor favor.

A forest of bone-white brambles stretches across the ashen waste, each thorn as long as a dagger and stained with old blood. Between the twisted branches, skeletal shapes stir and rattle. Somewhere deep within, a hag waits in her nest of ossified roots, bargaining in the only currencies that matter here: blood and memory.

Animated skeletons (AC 13, 45 HP, multiattack). Grapple traps: DC 16 Athletics, 2d6 piercing/round.

Hunger of Hadar / Black Tentacles in narrow passages.

DC 15 Nature/Survival halves travel time through the brambles.

Key NPC

Red Ruth — Night hag information broker. Demands blood or memories as payment. Ruthless negotiator but keeps her bargains. DC 18 Persuasion for a reduced price.

A vast mirror of black ice rises from a frozen crater, its surface rippling like dark water despite the infernal heat that surrounds it. The air here bites with supernatural cold, and frost patterns form words in Infernal that shift and change before they can be read. An archdevil's power lingers here, watching, waiting for a bargain to be struck.

4 ice devils + bone devil lieutenant guard the approach. DC 15 Acrobatics on slick surfaces or fall prone.

Rigorath (cambion) — Mephistopheles' intermediary. Unctuous, precise, dangerous. Demands blood or 5 soul coins. DC 20 Deception to extract extra information without additional payment.

Blood Pay
Arkhan's Tower / Monument to Tiamat
Choose 1+ blood pay • Retrieve Tiamat's blood
12–20 hours
What You Can Do Here
  • Retrieve Vial of Tiamat's blood
  • Fight Arkhan (CR 16, 221 HP)
  • Negotiate alternate sacrifice DC 18 Persuasion
  • Stealth-steal the vial DC 20 Stealth
  • Sacrifice an innocent (Lulu? Krikendolt?)
▶
Five colossal dragon heads carved from volcanic glass crown the monument, each maw agape with frozen fury. At its base, a fortified tower bristles with draconic banners and the stench of reptilian musk. Dragons circle above like vultures, and the ground trembles with the restless power of the Dragon Queen's memorial.
Goal

Retrieve a Vial of Tiamat's blood — one of the ingredients needed.

Approach

Young/wyrmling dragons attack on approach. Krull (tortle priest) arrives on adult white dragon Obatala to stop the fighting and parley.

Key NPCs

Arkhan the Cruel — Dragonborn oathbreaker paladin of Tiamat. Wields the Hand of Vecna. Demands an innocent sacrifice. CR 16, 221 HP.

Krull — Tortle death cleric. Voice of reason. Rides Obatala (adult white dragon).

Torogar — Arkhan's loyal half-dragon champion.

Arkhan's Demand

Sacrifice of an innocent (Lulu? Unicorn from Demon Zapper? Krikendolt?). DC 18 Persuasion for an alternate sacrifice.

Alternative — Combat

Fight Arkhan (CR 16, 221 HP) + Torogar + Krull + manticore. Remove abishai to even the odds. DC 20 Stealth to steal the vial instead.

Reward

Vial of Tiamat's blood.

Choose 1+ blood pay • Empyrean blood
12–20 hours
What You Can Do Here
  • Obtain empyrean blood
  • Persuade the trapped empyrean DC 16
  • Slay a dragon to restore empyrean's form

▶
A desolate grave mound rises from the scorched earth, marked by a single cracked obelisk that hums with celestial energy long since corrupted. Chained to the monument by invisible bonds, a wretched spined devil paces — but its eyes burn with a light far too noble for its twisted form.
Goal

Obtain empyrean blood.

Encounter

An empyrean, trapped in the form of a spined devil. DC 16 Persuasion to negotiate. Needs a dragon slain to restore its true form and give blood freely.

Complication

Third party hunting the empyrean — warlords, glabrezu + tanarukks converge on the location.

Reward

Mote of Heavenly Light (H) — 2d6 radiant + advantage on Wis saves.

What You Can Do Here
  • Obtain balor blood from imprisoned Ubbalux
  • Convince Krikendolt to swap places DC 14

▶
A towering obelisk of jet-black stone pulses with veins of molten crimson. The air around it vibrates with contained fury, and the ground splits in hairline fractures that glow with infernal heat. Something ancient and terrible is imprisoned within, and it wants out.
Goal

Obtain balor blood. A balor (Ubbalux) is imprisoned inside the obelisk; someone must take its place for the blood to be extracted.

Solution

Convince Krikendolt (at the Spawning Trees) to swap places. DC 14 Persuasion.

DC 18 Arcana for the binding ritual. DC 16 Deception to trick Ubbalux into cooperating.

Complication

Freed Ubbalux (balor with 4 exhaustion levels) may attack instead of cooperating. Weakened but still extremely dangerous.

Choose 1+ blood pay • Pit fiend ichor
14–24 hours
What You Can Do Here
  • Obtain pit fiend ichor
  • Dam the River Styx with shattersticks
  • Wait 24h for Shummrath to reform

▶
The River Styx flows sluggishly through a deep canyon here, its waters a nauseating swirl of soul-stuff and liquid misery. At the bottom of a blackened pit, something vast and barely-formed stirs in the muck — the dissolved remains of a pit fiend, endlessly reforming and dissolving in the cursed current.
Goal

Obtain pit fiend ichor. Dam the River Styx with shattersticks, then wait 24h for Shummrath to reform enough to extract ichor.

Requirements

Shattersticks from the Wandering Emporium (salamander artificer). DC 15 Strength for placement. DC 17 Con save vs toxic fumes.

Complication

Weakened pit fiend (4 exhaustion levels) may fight when it becomes sentient enough during reformation.

Reward

Hellfire Flail (H) — +1, 2d6 fire, 1/day fireball (4th level) if evil.

Stone steps descend into darkness beneath a crumbling monument to fallen knights. The walls are carved with scenes of mounted warriors charging into Hell itself, their faces set with terrible determination. The air is cold — impossibly cold for Avernus — and the distant sound of armored footsteps echoes from nowhere and everywhere.
Encounters

Upscaled undead: 2 deathlock masterminds + 10 zombies each. Wights and ghosts haunt the deeper chambers.

Key NPC

Olanthius — Death knight, former Hellrider commander. Tormented by guilt. Can explain Zariel's true motives and her fall. Directs party to Bel for an alliance against Zariel.

Task

Free Kostchtchie (at the Maw) to create a distraction that draws Zariel's forces away.

Haruman's Hill
Need info from 2+ sources • Haruman confrontation
What You Can Do Here
  • Confront Haruman (fights to the death)
  • Commune with souls in iron trees DC 15 Religion
  • Get sibriex location from Jander Sunstar

▶
Iron trees dot the hillside like a grotesque orchard, their metal branches hung with screaming souls twisted into agonized shapes. At the summit, a figure in tarnished plate armor stands watch over his domain of eternal punishment, a narzugon who once rode beside Zariel as a mortal paladin.
Encounters

Hellwasp Nest nearby (optional side encounter). Haruman + narzugon forces on the hill itself.

Combat

Haruman fights to the death. When slain, reverts to human paladin corpse. No soul remains — must find a sibriex to extract memories from the corpse.

Key NPC

Jander Sunstar — Vampire, trapped among the iron trees. Can hint at the sibriex location needed to extract Haruman's memories.

Iron Trees

Still hold screaming souls. DC 15 Religion to commune with the trapped spirits for fragments of information.

The forge burns with an intensity that makes Avernus itself seem cool by comparison. Massive anvils ring with the blows of fire giant smiths, and rivers of molten adamantine flow through channels carved into the obsidian floor. Above it all, the presence of a fallen pit fiend watches — calculating, patient, seething with ambition.
Test of Worthiness

4 fire giants. Bel watches from the shadows to judge the party's combat prowess. Must be defeated to earn an audience.

Key NPC

Bel — Former Archduke of Avernus, deposed by Zariel. Pit fiend schemer. Hates Zariel and will support the party through telepathic deal. Can suggest seeking a sibriex or the Arches of Ulloch for the rod location.

Task

Retrieve adamantine rods from the Wrecked Flying Fortress for Bel's war effort.

▶
A wound in the earth opens into a lightless abyss, its edges scorched and frozen simultaneously. From the depths, an inhuman howling rises and falls like breathing — the rage of a demon lord imprisoned far from the Abyss, chained by Zariel's power and left to rot in impotent fury.

Endgame
Arches of Ulloch
Gateway to the Bleeding Citadel • Sphinx challenge
What You Can Do Here
  • Pass the Nine Layers challenge DC 15 Int
  • Use Gargauth's knowledge (at a price)
  • Fight Ulloch the Sphinx if 3+ wrong answers
  • Reach the Bleeding Citadel (only path)
▶
Nine stone arches stand in a circle upon a plateau of polished obsidian, each carved with scenes depicting a different layer of the Nine Hells. Between them, space itself seems to fold and ripple. At the center, an androsphinx reclines with terrible patience, its leonine eyes fixed on the approaching mortals with the calm certainty of something that has never been wrong.
Guardian

Ulloch the Sphinx (androsphinx) — Administers the Nine Hells quiz challenge. Ancient, inscrutable, bound to this duty.

Mechanics

Each wrong answer: Ulloch uses Roar ability. 3 wrong answers = full combat against the sphinx.

Nine Layers Challenge

Party is teleported to each layer and must identify it while fighting/surviving layer-specific hazards. DC 15 Int (Arcana/History/Religion) per layer. Gargauth can help (at a price — increased influence).

The only way to reach the Bleeding Citadel — Asmodeus hid it with powerful magic. There is no shortcut.

Endgame
Wrecked Flying Fortress
Mini dungeon crawl • Bel's adamantine rods
What You Can Do Here
  • Retrieve adamantine rods for Bel
  • Solve safe puzzle (combo: 6-6-6)
  • Fight 6 vrocks + necrotic remorhaz (CR 11)
  • Navigate firestorm approach DC 20 Survival
  • Confront Princeps Kovic (warlord)
▶
The shattered hulk of an infernal warship lies half-buried in the ash wastes, its once-proud wings of beaten iron now crumpled and scorched. Firestorms rage around the wreckage, and the screeching of vrocks echoes from the broken hull. Something valuable was locked away in the ship's vault before it fell, and the fortress has become its own tomb.
Firestorm Approach

Group DC 20 Survival to navigate. Dispel magic or flight to bypass.

Encounters

6 vrocks guard the exterior. Interior: bone whelks, necrotic remorhaz (CR 11, 195 HP).

Safe Puzzle

Combination: 6-6-6. Riddle: “Three numbers the same, but double in sum...”

3 failed attempts awakens the remorhaz from hibernation.

Complication

Princeps Kovic (warlord) arrives after the party finishes looting — wants the adamantine rods for herself.

“The geography of Avernus is a lie told by a plane that wants you lost.”
