Blood Pay Locations
Blood Pay
Arkhan's Tower / Monument to Tiamat
Choose 1+ blood pay • Retrieve Tiamat's blood
Travel

12–20 hours by war machine.

Goal

Retrieve a Vial of Tiamat's blood.

Approach

Young/wyrmling dragons attack on approach. Krull (tortle priest) arrives on adult white dragon Obatala to stop fighting.

Arkhan's Demand

Sacrifice of an innocent (Lulu? Unicorn from Demon Zapper? Krikendolt?).

Alternative — Combat

Fight Arkhan (CR 16, 221 HP) + Torogar + Krull + manticore. Remove abishai to even odds.

DC 18 Persuasion for alternate sacrifice. DC 20 Stealth to steal.

Reward

Vial of Tiamat's blood.

Choose 1+ blood pay • Empyrean blood

12–20 hours by war machine.

Goal

Obtain empyrean blood.

Obelisk (Ubbalux)
Choose 1+ blood pay • Balor blood
Travel

14–22 hours by war machine.

Goal

Obtain balor blood. Balor imprisoned; someone must take its place.

Solution

Convince Krikendolt (at Spawning Trees) to swap places. DC 14 Persuasion.

Freed Ubbalux (balor with 4 exhaustion levels) may attack instead of cooperating.

DC 18 Arcana for ritual. DC 16 Deception to trick Ubbalux.

14–24 hours by war machine.

Obtain pit fiend ichor. Dam River Styx with shattersticks, wait 24h for Shummrath to reform.

Demon lord prison • Strategic distraction
Purpose

Party knows this exists. Freeing the demon lord distracts Zariel. Olanthius may require this before revealing info.

Endgame
Arches of Ulloch
Gateway to the Bleeding Citadel • Sphinx challenge
Guardian

Ulloch the Sphinx (androsphinx). Nine Hells quiz challenge.

Mechanics

Each wrong answer: Ulloch uses Roar ability. 3 wrong = fight the sphinx.

Nine Layers Challenge

Party teleported to each layer, must identify it while fighting/surviving hazards. DC 15 Int (Arcana/History/Religion) per layer. Gargauth can help (at a price).

The only way to reach the Bleeding Citadel — Asmodeus hid it with powerful magic.

Endgame
Wrecked Flying Fortress
Mini dungeon crawl • Bel's adamantine rods
Firestorm Approach

Group DC 20 Survival. Dispel magic or fly to avoid.

Encounters

6 vrocks outside. Interior: bone whelks, necrotic remorhaz (CR 11, 195 HP).

Safe Puzzle

Combination: 6-6-6. Riddle: “Three numbers the same, but double in sum...”

3 failed attempts awakens the remorhaz.

Complication

Princeps Kovic (warlord) arrives after looting — wants the rods.

“The geography of Avernus is a lie told by a plane that wants you lost.”
