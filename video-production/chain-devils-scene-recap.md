# Chain Devils Encounter — Actual Session Recap

**Source:** User narration 2026-04-24
**Campaign context:** Session 3 approach, Palace of Gore infiltration via drainage tunnels

## What actually happened

The party approached Bitter Breath's base. They entered a network of drainage tunnels. Aurora cast **Arcane Eye** to scout ahead — and the floating magical eye revealed that multiple chain devils were lying in wait inside the tunnels.

Only **3 chain devils** approached the party initially. Then **2 more** came after.

The party's tactic: they used the **Lady Vengeance** (their Gondian-Portyr Hellship, AC 17, 350 HP, with Arcane Cannons) to fire a **force blast** down the tunnel. The blast collapsed the tunnel to funnel and draw the devils into a kill-zone.

Then they engaged with their personal abilities in close combat.
