# CHAIN DEVILS — SCREENPLAY v2

**Format:** Film screenplay with spatial blocking
**Target:** ~60 seconds final cut, 15 shots
**Style:** Dark fantasy D&D Avernus, no voice acting, parchment text cards for narration
**Spatial grammar:** 180-degree rule enforced — party LEFT of frame, devils RIGHT, Lady Vengeance UPSTREAM (behind party)

---

## BLOCK DIAGRAM (sustained across all shots)

```
           (UPSTREAM — Lady Vengeance parked)
                         │
                         ▼
         PARTY ───── TUNNEL ────── DEVILS ───── (DOWNSTREAM — Palace of Gore)
         (LEFT)    (CENTER)       (RIGHT)
```

Every shot honors this: camera can pan, dolly, or change lens — but PARTY stays on screen-left, DEVILS stay on screen-right.

## SCENE: INT. PALACE OF GORE — DRAINAGE TUNNELS — AVERNUS — NIGHT

---

### SHOT 1 — EXT ESTABLISHING WIDE [3.5s, STILL]

> **EXT. THE TUNNEL MOUTH.** Wide silhouette. The LADY VENGEANCE — armored hell-warship, engraved arcane cannons along her flanks, Aether Harpoon mounted on the bow — parked at the mouth of a cavernous drainage tunnel cut into black Avernus stone. Orange-red hellforge light glows from her engines. A river of dark blood trickles out of the tunnel past her hull. Sulphurous amber sky behind.
>
> **Parchment card (lower third):** *"Beneath the Palace of Gore."*

---

### SHOT 2 — MEDIUM: AURORA CASTS ARCANE EYE [3s, STILL]

> **INT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** AURORA (left side of frame, three-quarter) stands at the tunnel entrance. DRENWAL and ASIMOV visible behind her in soft focus. Her right hand is raised, silver-violet fey magic condensing at her fingertips. A small translucent EYE CONSTRUCT begins to form in the air before her — spectral eyeball shot through with silver veins, a living sigil of scouting magic.
>
> Her green eyes are closed in concentration. The quirky smirk is gone. She is serious now.

---

### SHOT 3 — POV: ARCANE EYE DRIFTS [5s, MOTION]

> **POV — ARCANE EYE.** The camera IS the floating spectral eye, drifting slowly forward down the corridor. Blood-lacquered walls slide past left and right. Distant hellforge braziers throw flickering red light on the gore-crusted floor. Knee-deep pool of ancient blood below. Distance ambient: chain rattles far ahead.
>
> Silver-violet arcane haze softens the edges of the frame — we are seeing through a spell, not a camera.

---

### SHOT 4 — POV REVEAL: CHAIN DEVILS [4.5s, MOTION]

> **POV — ARCANE EYE.** The corridor opens into a chamber. The eye glides forward. In the flickering red light, FIVE CHAIN DEVILS stand in waiting — iron-wrapped gaunt humanoids, skull-hooded faces, animated spiked chains coiling in the air around them like nesting serpents. They have not noticed the eye. One turns its head slowly.
>
> Smash-cut close as the devil's eyes snap open directly at the camera — FROZEN FRAME.

---

### SHOT 5 — CU: AURORA BREAKS CONCENTRATION [2.5s, STILL]

> **INT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** EXTREME CLOSE-UP on Aurora. Her eyes open violently — jade-green irises dilating. The forming arcane eye sigil at her fingertips dissipates into silver sparks. Breath caught. She has seen what was ahead. She turns her head sharply toward Drenwal and Asimov (off-frame left).
>
> **Parchment card:** *"Five of them. In waiting."*

---

### SHOT 6 — MEDIUM: PARTY ON LADY VENGEANCE DECK [3.5s, STILL]

> **EXT. LADY VENGEANCE DECK — TUNNEL MOUTH.** Medium three-shot. AURORA (left-of-frame) has just reported. DRENWAL (center) stands grim, one gauntleted hand resting on his shield, the other on the brass-and-bone ARCANE CANNON mounted on the ship's rail — the cannon is already aimed down the tunnel. ASIMOV (right-of-frame, in shadow) is already half-faded into the dark, Soul Capacitor pulsing violet at his belt, daggers not yet drawn.
>
> Drenwal gives a single nod. Decision made. The trap is set.

---

### SHOT 7 — WIDE: CANNON FIRES FORCE BLAST [5s, MOTION]

> **EXT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** Side angle. The Lady Vengeance's arcane cannon RECOILS with a crack of blue-white magical energy. A blinding FORCE BLAST — spherical wavefront of translucent sapphire light crackling with runic discharge — launches down the tunnel at incredible speed. The camera stays outside, watching the blast CONSUME the tunnel mouth. Dust and debris start erupting as the pressure wave hits.
>
> Motion: cannon fires, blast travels, hits tunnel, shockwave rolls back outward.

---

### SHOT 8 — INSIDE TUNNEL: IMPACT & COLLAPSE [5s, MOTION]

> **INT. DEEP TUNNEL.** The force blast hits the waiting chain devils — the sapphire wavefront slams them backward, some are thrown against the walls. Stone cracks. Bone-ribbed ceiling collapses downward in chunks. Blood-pool ripples violently. The tunnel seals behind the devils — cutting off their retreat.
>
> Three figures are still standing in the dust. Two more are stumbling up from behind, emerging from a side alcove the collapse didn't reach.

---

### SHOT 9 — WIDE: 3 CHAIN DEVILS EMERGE THROUGH DUST [3.5s, STILL]

> **INT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** The dust is clearing. THREE CHAIN DEVILS stand in silhouette at the tunnel mouth (right-of-frame), chains dragging along the stone floor, hellforge embers crackling along the barbs. The force blast has enraged rather than defeated them.
>
> They advance.

---

### SHOT 10 — WIDE TWO-SHOT: DRENWAL CASTS WORD OF RADIANCE [5s, MOTION]

> **INT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** Wide two-shot. DRENWAL (center-left) plants his shield forward, left hand raised — and a COLUMN OF PURE GOLDEN LATHANDRIAN SUNLIGHT erupts from his position, radiant rays lancing down the corridor toward the advancing devils. Same frame: the THREE CHAIN DEVILS (right-of-frame) stagger as the holy light hits them, their pale skin smoking and blistering, chains clattering.
>
> Cause and effect in ONE frame. The cleric's spell and its impact, no cut between.

---

### SHOT 11 — MEDIUM: AURORA NOCKS ARROW [2.5s, STILL]

> **INT. TUNNEL MOUTH.** AURORA (left-of-frame, profile view looking right toward devils) draws the MOONBOW smoothly, starlight bowstring pulled to her cheek. A radiant silver-gold arrow of pure starlight MANIFESTS on the string as she draws — True Strike channeled through the bow. Her jade eyes are locked on target.
>
> Behind her, Drenwal's radiant column is fading, the nearest chain devil wounded but standing.

---

### SHOT 12 — WIDE: ARROW FLIGHT + IMPACT [5s, MOTION]

> **INT. TUNNEL.** Wide horizontal shot. Aurora RELEASES — the radiant arrow streaks from left to right across the full length of the frame, leaving a silver-gold comet trail. It IMPACTS the center chain devil — punches clean through its chest in a burst of radiant light. The devil arches backward, chains falling slack, already dying as its body begins to collapse.
>
> One continuous arc — launch to impact in one uncut motion.

---

### SHOT 13 — MEDIUM: ASIMOV RISES FROM SHADOW BEHIND DEVIL [4s, MOTION]

> **INT. TUNNEL.** Behind the remaining two advancing chain devils (right-of-frame), in a pool of deep shadow, ASIMOV rises silently from the darkness — hood up, face invisible, Soul Capacitor pulsing violet at his belt. Violet psionic mist coils around both gloved hands and condenses into TWO TRANSLUCENT VIOLET PSYCHIC DAGGERS.
>
> The devils have not yet noticed him. He is perfectly positioned for the sneak attack.

---

### SHOT 14 — MEDIUM: SNEAK ATTACK STRIKE + DEVIL FALLS [5s, MOTION]

> **INT. TUNNEL.** Asimov drives BOTH violet psychic daggers forward and UP into the back of the nearest chain devil — a burst of violet psionic light erupts through the devil's ribcage from within. The devil convulses, chains going slack, and collapses forward into the blood-pool.
>
> Behind Asimov, the fifth chain devil turns, raising its barbed chains to strike — but we do not see the strike land. Cut.

---

### SHOT 15 — WIDE AFTERMATH + ENDING CARD [4.5s, STILL]

> **INT. TUNNEL — AFTERMATH.** Wide shot. The party stands in line across the left side of the tunnel — DRENWAL center with shield lowered, AURORA left with Moonbow held low, ASIMOV right, violet mist dissipating from his hands. Across from them, the blood-pool is crusted with fallen chain devils, loose chains, and the faint remaining silver glow of radiant damage.
>
> Down the tunnel, past the slain, a faint distant orange glow — the PALACE OF GORE. The way forward is open.
>
> **Parchment card (fades in):** *"The way to Bitter Breath was open."*

## SPATIAL GRAMMAR RULES (enforced per shot)

1. **Party always left or left-of-center.**
2. **Devils always right or right-of-center.**
3. **Lady Vengeance always upstream / behind party.**
4. **Palace of Gore always downstream / far end of tunnel.**
5. **Arcane Eye POV shots drift forward (into frame depth).**
6. **Force blast travels left-to-right.**
7. **Radiant arrow travels left-to-right (Aurora → devil).**
8. **Asimov's shadow position is flanking — comes from behind/right of devils.**
9. **Aspect ratio: 16:9 only, no 1:1 shots this cut.**
10. **Lighting: Avernus amber backlight in corridor, cool silver for spell effects, warm gold for Drenwal's Lathandrian light, violet for Asimov's psionic.**

## CHARACTER REFERENCE STRATEGY

- Each shot references **the PREVIOUS scene shot** as its image seed (continuity anchor for light/blocking/lens).
- Key character beats (Aurora casting, Drenwal casting, Asimov strike) additionally reference the character canon for identity lock.
- Devil appearances reference Shot 9 (the dust-clearing reveal) as the devil canon anchor — so all subsequent devil shots look like the same three devils.
