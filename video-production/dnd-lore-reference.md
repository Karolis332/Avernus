## KYTON (Chain Devil) — Monster Manual p.72

**What they are:** Lawful evil fiends. Medium-size. Muscular humanoid silhouette wrapped in barbed chains.

**Canonical visual description (MM p.72 verbatim extract):**
> "A kyton appears as a tall, muscular humanoid wrapped in heavy chains with barbed hooks driven into its flesh. Its face is covered by a simple mask — sometimes a metal skull-cap with holes, sometimes a ragged piece of cloth, sometimes the flayed face of another creature."

**Key visual rules — what a kyton IS:**
- Iron-wrapped humanoid body (NOT demon, NOT horned, NOT fiery-red)
- Heavy RUSTED barbed chains wound around them, chains DRIVEN into their own flesh as piercings (S&M-adjacent body-horror aesthetic)
- Pale grey-white SCARRED flesh visible between chain wraps, pocked with small wounds where chains bite
- Face HIDDEN BY A MASK. Options:
  - Simple metal skullcap / headcage with eye-holes
  - Ragged cloth / burlap wrap
  - Flayed face of a slain creature worn as mask
- NO horns, NO wings, NO bat-wings, NO cloven hooves, NO red demon skin, NO tail
- Color palette: cold rust-iron, grey-white flesh, black blood, muted
- Size: human-height or slightly taller, lean-muscular not beastly

**Combat behavior:**
- **Multiattack:** Two chain attacks per turn (+8, reach 10 ft, 2d6+4 piercing)
- **Animate Chains (1/day):** Causes up to 4 separate chains within 60 ft to animate autonomously — each acts independently on their initiative. Each animated chain: AC 20, HP 20, +8 attack, 3d6+4 piercing, can restrain target on hit. Creates a forest of writhing spiked chains around the kyton.
- **Unnerving Mask:** When a kyton targets a creature that has seen something die, the kyton's mask briefly transforms into the face of a creature the target has slain — victim DC 14 WIS or frightened 1 min.
- Chains whip and lash — they are NOT idle. At rest the chains coil slowly like serpents. In combat they SNAP outward.

**Atmosphere:** uncanny, industrial-dungeon horror, body-horror, cold. NOT hellish-demonic. Think: Clive Barker's cenobites, Silent Hill's Pyramid Head, not typical fantasy devils.

## LIGHT DOMAIN CLERIC (Drenwal) — PHB + 2024 PHB

**Combat role:** Front-line support. Wears medium armor (chain/scale) + shield. Typically carries mace, warhammer, or sword. Holds holy symbol (Helm's open eye or Lathander's sun rising) in off-hand or embedded in shield.

**Drenwal specifically:**
- Hellrider of Elturel — red and ivory vestments with silver Celtic knotwork, crimson-lined cloak, silver-embossed breastplate
- Wields the **Shield of the Hidden Lord** (Gargauth item) — red-lacquered, silver diamond framework, central eye-in-sun, with grinning devil face subtly carved into the silverwork
- Has leather spellcasting gauntlet on casting hand
- Worships Helm (has never heard the god's voice), tempted by Gargauth via the Shield

**Key spells and how they LOOK when cast:**

| Spell | Visual |
|---|---|
| **Word of Radiance** (cantrip) | **Small 5-ft RADIUS BURST of radiant light from caster's position** — each target within 5 ft takes 1d6 radiant on a failed Con save. Caster is the center. Gold-white flash, not a beam or column. |
| **Sacred Flame** (cantrip) | Single-target column of descending flame from above on one target within 60 ft, 1d8 radiant. Pillar of light from the sky. |
| **Guiding Bolt** (1st lvl) | 120-ft BOLT of divine radiant light — 4d6 radiant, next attack vs target has advantage. Bright flare in flight, impact burst. |
| **Spiritual Weapon** (2nd lvl) | Floating radiant weapon (typically mace/hammer), moves independently to strike. |
| **Spirit Guardians** (3rd lvl) | 15-ft aura of translucent spectral guardians around cleric, 3d8 radiant/necrotic per creature per turn. |
| **Flame Strike** (5th lvl) | 10-ft radius × 40-ft tall cylinder of divine flame from sky, 4d6 fire + 4d6 radiant. |
| **Radiance of the Dawn** (Channel Divinity, Light domain) | **30-ft radius BURST centered on cleric, dispels magical darkness, 2d10 + cleric level radiant damage to each hostile within**. Big golden wave. |
| **Warding Flare** (reaction, Light domain) | When attacked, cleric flares divine light in attacker's face — attacker has disadvantage. Single small flash. |

**For the chain devils scene:**
- "Word of Radiance" as stated in the recap = SMALL 5ft burst around Drenwal. More like a detonation of holy light at his feet than a lance. Great for when devils have closed to melee range.
- If we want the BIG column-of-light drama we should call it **Radiance of the Dawn** (Channel Divinity, 30-ft burst) — mechanically justified, visually spectacular, correctly tagged.

## ARCHFEY WARLOCK (Aurora) — PHB + 2024 PHB

**Combat role:** Sustained ranged DPS. Low HP, high damage-per-round via Eldritch Blast or Moonbow. Uses positioning + invocations for utility.

**Aurora specifically:**
- Astral elf astrally-touched to the Feywild
- Patron: The Moonkite — celestial-fey being (formerly caged in Palace of Gore)
- Pact weapon: **Moonbow of Celestial Warding** (bow + wand fused by Moonkite antlers)
  - +2 shortbow, arrows manifest as silver-white STARLIGHT bolts (1d6+2 piercing + 1d4 radiant)
  - Spellcasting focus
  - Holds 7 charges: Magic Missile, Shield, Counterspell
  - 1/LR: Banishment to Feywild
  - Moonkite Bond: can communicate with Moonkite once per long rest
- Familiar: **Whiskerbright** — Feywild cat, currently in IMP form (Avernus-adapted), keeps cat personality
- Wears legendary **Quiver of Elemental Chaos** (Griffon's Saddlebag) on hip — 5-hole lid, 4 element buttons (Air/Earth/Fire/Water), panels depicting planes

**Key spells and how they LOOK:**

| Spell | Visual |
|---|---|
| **Eldritch Blast** (cantrip, Agonizing Blast) | Up to 4 beams of violet-green force, 1d10 each + CHA mod, 120-ft range. |
| **True Strike** (2024 cantrip) | **She makes a weapon attack as part of the cantrip** — on hit, weapon damage + extra 1d6 (scaling) of radiant / cold / psychic / thunder / fire / lightning (Aurora picks at cast). For Moonbow: fires a normal starlight arrow, the arrow is WREATHED in radiant energy on impact. NOT a separate arrow — it's her Moonbow shot enhanced. |
| **Hex** (1st lvl) | Curse mark appears over target, dark purple fey sigil hovering. Extra 1d6 necrotic on each hit. |
| **Misty Escape** (feat, 7th) | When damaged, can vanish in silver fey mist + teleport 60 ft, invisible 1 round. |
| **Fey Presence** (feat, 1st) | 10-ft cube from her — targets DC or charmed OR frightened 1 turn. Silver-purple glamour aura. |
| **Counterspell** (3rd lvl, via Moonbow) | Point Moonbow at enemy caster, silver ripple shatters their spell. |

**For chain devils scene:**
- She uses **True Strike + Moonbow** — fires a starlight arrow that hits with both physical (piercing) and radiant rider. Radiant damage is extra-punishing vs fiends.
- Moonkite Bond gives her a sense of the tunnel layout (spell plus item synergy).

## SOULKNIFE ROGUE (Asimov) — Tasha's Cauldron of Everything

**Combat role:** Burst damage via sneak attack + psychic blades, stealth infiltration, psionic utility.

**Asimov specifically:**
- Interdimensional bounty hunter for a soul-trade SYNDICATE
- Patron/handler: **Rasheem** — efreeti (djinn/genie) trapped in lamp, syndicate operative
- Core kit: **Soul Capacitor** — his working magic item
- Hood + dark coat + leather bandoliers + psionic-rune bracers

**The Soul Capacitor (campaign-custom item — canonical):**
- **An ornate bronze oil lamp worn ON HIS BELT**
- Laced with crystalline violet veins that pulse along the bronze surface like circuitry
- Violet smoke curls from the lamp's spout constantly
- When Rasheem speaks, the lamp glows brighter and smoke-face manifests above it
- Stores consumed souls for bonus powers (Medium souls tier active: Psionic Surge, Soul Shiv, Memory Siphon, Soul Mask, Psionic Echo)
- **NEVER in hand in combat — the lamp is a belt item, it stays on his belt**
- Asimov's hands are for daggers, the lamp is NOT a weapon

**Psychic Blades (Soulknife's core feature):**
- **Made of PURE PSIONIC ENERGY, not crystal, not metal**
- Translucent VIOLET/BLUE shimmering blades that materialize in his hands instantly
- Flicker and waver slightly — like smoke shaped into dagger form
- Can be dismissed and re-formed as part of any attack
- Thrown psychic blades dissipate after hitting (1d6 psychic, range 60/120 ft)
- Each blade is the length of a long knife / short sword
- **Key visual error to avoid:** solid crystal daggers are WRONG. Must be translucent smoke-and-light energy shapes.

**Psychic Power Die** (d10 at level 11):
- **Psi-Bolstered Knack:** add die to failed ability check
- **Psychic Whispers:** telepathic comms (no verbal)
- **Homing Strikes:** add die to attack roll if missed
- **Soul Blades:** add die to damage or to-hit on psychic blade attacks

**Combat patterns for Asimov:**
1. **Cunning Action** (bonus action: Dash / Disengage / Hide) to set up sneak attack
2. **Hide in darkness** (Stealth + Prof) — rogues are built around this
3. **Sneak Attack** applies to psychic blade attacks when he has advantage OR an ally is within 5ft of target (Advantage-source: shadow, ambush, Homing Strikes)
4. **Sneak Attack damage** at 11th level: 6d6 extra psychic on hit
5. **Throw psychic blade** at range for ranged sneak attack opportunities

**Rasheem appearance (when manifesting):**
- Emerges as violet smoke from the Soul Capacitor lamp
- Forms into an efreeti-like figure: tall, theatrical, flowing dark robes woven from psionic smoke
- Purple aura surrounds him
- Voice is heard in Asimov's head (telepathic); other PCs hear it too when he wants them to
- NEVER fights directly — he's a handler, not a combatant

## SPATIAL / NARRATIVE CORRECTIONS FOR v3

**What was wrong in v2 renders:**
1. Chain devils rendered as horned red-skinned demons with lava/fire (MM says grey-pale, iron-wrapped, masked, NOT demons)
2. Soul Capacitor lamp missing or misplaced (belt item, not in hand, not absent)
3. Asimov's psychic blades rendered as solid violet crystal (should be translucent psionic energy)
4. Drenwal's "Word of Radiance" rendered as huge lancing column (actually a 5-ft radial burst; if we want the column we should re-label as Radiance of the Dawn or Flame Strike)

**What must be explicit in v3 prompts:**
- Kytons: "iron-wrapped humanoid figures, NOT horned demons, NOT red-skinned, NO wings, pale scarred grey-white flesh, heavy rusted barbed chains piercing into their own flesh as body-modifications, faces HIDDEN by simple metal skullcap masks with eye-holes only, multiple animated chains coiling around them like hunting serpents"
- Soul Capacitor: "An ornate bronze oil lamp WORN ON HIS BELT at hip-level, NOT in his hand — the lamp has crystalline violet veins along its bronze surface that pulse like living circuitry, violet smoke curling constantly from the lamp's spout"
- Psychic blades: "Translucent purple-violet SHIMMERING energy blades formed from pure psionic mist — NOT solid crystal, NOT metal. They flicker and waver slightly like smoke shaped into dagger form. Glowing inner light."
- Drenwal's big radiant burst: re-label as "Radiance of the Dawn channel divinity — a 30-foot radius burst of pure golden Lathandrian sunlight centered on Drenwal, radiant wave washing outward through the corridor"
