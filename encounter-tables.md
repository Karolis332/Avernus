## Warlord Encounter Profiles

### Bloodbath the Drinker of Gore *(DEFEATED)*
- Absorbed by Rasheem. No longer a field threat. Can be interrogated.

### Bitter Breath, Brother of Bloodbath (CR 16-17)
- **Forces:** Hobgoblins + Bloodbath's remaining maw demons/merregons (uneasy mix)
- **Behavior:** Smaller but stronger than Bloodbath. More cunning, less reckless.
- **At half HP:** Fights harder — refuses to fall like his brother did
- **Dialogue:** Cold, bitter. Resents living in Bloodbath's shadow. Wants to prove superiority.
- **Lair:** The Palace of Gore — holds the **Moonkite's prison**
- **Directions:** Follow the river of blood, follow the smell of rotting guts

### Smiler the Defiler (Recurring)
- **Behavior:** Ambush, taunt, flee. Leaves traps.
- **Cannot be pinned down.** Escapes on war machine.
- **Useful as:** Information conduit when party is stuck
- **Bitter Rivals adventure:** Asks party to help fight Bitter Breath's hobgoblins

### Princeps Kovic (CR 13-15)
- **Forces:** Merregons, snipers, shield walls, war machines, nets
- **Behavior:** Capture, not kill. Disciplined formation tactics.
- **DC 17 Cha** to talk down. Can become uneasy ally.
- **Wants:** Adamantine rods, prisoners, intelligence

### Rust Queen
- **Forces:** Rust monsters, meazels, barbed devils
- **Behavior:** Acid pools, corrosive fog, weapon degradation
- **DC 17 Dex** to avoid war machine accidents in her territory

## Upscaled Encounter Templates

### Standard Devil Patrol (CR ~11 total)
- 1 chain devil (CR 8, 85 HP) + 2 bearded devils (CR 3, 52 HP each) + 3 spined devils (CR 2, 22 HP each)
- Add barbed devil reinforcement at round 3

### Elite Warlord Fight (CR ~14)
- 1 boss (narzugon or homebrew) + 2 bodyguards (barbed devils or merregons)
- Environmental hazard: lava flow, collapsing structure, or war machine fuel leak
- Boss gets 1-2 legendary actions

### Moral Dilemma Combat
- Present civilians, prisoners, or soul coins mid-fight
- "The devil offers to stop fighting if you hand over 3 soul coins and Lulu"
- "The prisoners will die in 3 rounds unless someone breaks the chains (DC 16 Athletics, full action)"
