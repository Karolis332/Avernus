# BITTER BREATH, BROTHER OF BLOODBATH

**Medium fiend (demon), chaotic evil**

---

**AC** 19 (natural armor) | **HP** 280 (buffed for party of 3 at level 11) | **Speed** 50 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 24 (+7) | 20 (+5) | 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) |

**Saving Throws** Str +13, Dex +11, Wis +9

**Damage Resistances** cold, lightning, nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** fire, poison
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned

**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 16
**Languages** Abyssal, Infernal; telepathy 120 ft.

**Challenge** 16 (15,000 XP)

---

## Traits

**Blood Frenzy.** Bitter Breath has advantage on melee attacks against creatures that have fewer than their hit point maximum.

**Evasive Gore.** When Bitter Breath is hit by an attack, he can use his reaction to halve the damage if he can see the attacker. He then moves up to 15 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Aura of Rot.** Any creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of Bitter Breath must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution save or be poisoned until the end of its next turn. While poisoned this way, the creature's speed is halved.

**Magic Resistance.** Bitter Breath has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Legendary Resistance (3/day).** If Bitter Breath fails a save, he can choose to succeed instead.

**Relentless (1/day).** If Bitter Breath is reduced to 0 hit points, he drops to 1 hit point instead.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Bitter Breath makes three Gutripper attacks, or two Gutripper attacks and one Gore Horns attack.

**Gore Horns.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 18 (2d10 + 7) piercing + 9 (2d8) necrotic. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity save or Bitter Breath moves to the opposite side of the target (no opportunity attack) and attacks again as part of the same action.

**Gutripper.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 16 (2d8 + 7) slashing + 7 (2d6) necrotic. On a critical hit, the target begins bleeding (takes 7 (2d6) necrotic damage at the start of each of its turns until it receives magical healing or someone uses an action to stanch the wound with a DC 15 Medicine check).

**Rotten Breath (Recharge 5–6).** Bitter Breath exhales a 40-foot cone of putrid, corrosive gas. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Constitution save, taking 36 (8d8) poison damage and 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. A creature that fails by 5 or more is also stunned until the end of its next turn.

---

## Bonus Actions

**Skirmisher.** Bitter Breath takes the Dash or Disengage action.

---

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

Bitter Breath can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

**Reposition.** Bitter Breath moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Gutripper Strike.** Bitter Breath makes one Gutripper attack.

**Flaying Rush (Costs 2 Actions).** Bitter Breath moves up to his speed. Each creature he passes within 5 feet of during this movement must make a DC 17 Dexterity save or take 14 (4d6) slashing damage. He can make one Gutripper attack at the end of this movement.

---

## Tactics

Bitter Breath fights nothing like his brother. Where Bloodbath stood his ground and drank blood, Bitter Breath never stops moving. He opens with **Rotten Breath** to stun and poison, then uses **Skirmisher** and his 50 ft. speed to dart between targets, focusing on wounded PCs with Blood Frenzy advantage. His legendary **Reposition** and **Flaying Rush** ensure he's never where the party expects. He uses **Evasive Gore** to punish attackers and reposition on reaction. **Relentless** means the party thinks he's down — and then he's not.

## Personality

Cold and bitter. Speaks in short, venomous sentences. Resents his brother's fame. Doesn't roar — he hisses. Doesn't boast — he dismisses.

**Sample lines:**
- *"My brother was loud. I am precise."*
- *"He drank blood like a fool. I let it drain slowly."*
- *"You killed him? No. You killed a drunk animal. I am something worse."*
- *"Smaller, they said. Always smaller. Let me show you what smaller means."*
- *(after Relentless triggers)* *"...No."*
