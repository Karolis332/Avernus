# BLOODBATH, DRINKER OF GORE

**Large fiend (demon), chaotic evil**

---

**AC** 18 | **HP** 210 (20d10 + 100) | **Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 22 (+6) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 11 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) |

**Saving Throws** Con +11, Wis +8, Cha +9

**Damage Resistances** cold, lightning, nonmagical weapons
**Damage Immunities** fire, poison
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned

**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Abyssal, Infernal; telepathy 120 ft.

**Challenge** 13 (10,000 XP)

---

## Traits

**Blood Frenzy.** Bloodbath has advantage on melee attacks against creatures that have fewer than their hit point maximum.

**Gore Drinker.** Whenever Bloodbath reduces a creature to 0 hit points, he regains 22 (4d10 + 2) hit points.

**Aura of Gore.** Any creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of Bloodbath must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom save or be frightened until the end of its next turn.

**Magic Resistance.** Bloodbath has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Legendary Resistance (3/day).** If Bloodbath fails a save, he can choose to succeed instead.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Bloodbath makes one Gore Horns attack and two Bloodied Cleaver attacks.

**Gore Horns.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d10 + 6) piercing + 7 (2d6) necrotic. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength save or be knocked prone. If the target is already prone, Bloodbath can move up to half his speed and gore the same target again.

**Bloodied Cleaver.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 16 (2d8 + 6) slashing + 7 (2d6) necrotic. Bloodbath regains hit points equal to the necrotic damage dealt.

**Blood Spray (Recharge 5–6).** Bloodbath exhales blood in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 18 Constitution save, taking 42 (12d6) necrotic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. A creature that fails this save is blinded until the end of its next turn.

**Summon Gore Minions (1/day).** Bloodbath summons 2d4 maw demons in unoccupied spaces he can see within 60 feet. They act on his initiative and last for 1 hour or until he dismisses them.

---

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

Bloodbath can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

**Blood Cleaver.** Bloodbath makes a Bloodied Cleaver attack.

**Rush of Gore.** Bloodbath moves up to his speed without provoking opportunity attacks, then uses Gore Horns.

**Drink Deep (Costs 2 Actions).** Bloodbath targets one bleeding creature within 15 feet of him that he can see. That creature must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution save, taking 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. Bloodbath regains hit points equal to half the damage dealt.
