```
Fort Knucklebone (hub)

    │   ├── Tower of Urm (Mordenkainen) — 10-16h
    │   ├── Bone Brambles (Red Ruth) — 8-14h
    │   └── Mirror of Mephistar (Mephistopheles) — 10-18h
    │
    ├── Blood Pay (choose 1+)
    │   ├── Arkhan's Tower / Monument to Tiamat — 12-20h
    │   ├── Uldrak's Grave — 12-20h
    │   ├── Obelisk (Ubbalux) — 14-22h
    │   └── Shummrath's Pit — 14-24h
    │
    ├── Fallen Three (need info from 2+)
    │   ├── Crypt of the Hellriders (Olanthius) — 8-12h
    │   ├── Haruman's Hill (Haruman) — varies
    │   └── Bel's Forge (Bel) — varies
    │
    └── Arches of Ulloch → Bleeding Citadel
```

## Travel Notes
- All times by war machine. Double on foot.
- Roll encounter table per travel segment
- Safe rest locations are RARE. Control pacing by limiting rests.
- Fuel: soul coins (24h each)
- Environmental: DC 15 Con save on arduous stretches or gain exhaustion

## Fort Knucklebone
- **Purpose:** Hub, neutral ground, war machine repairs, trading
- **Features:** Kenku tinkerers, the Well (potable water), soul coin economy
- **Downtime options:** Fix war machine (1-2h), trade with Maggie, visit Well, gamble at Arcade
- **Loot table (d6):** 2 soul coins / Infernal Repair Kit / Maggie's Talisman (tongues 1x) / Salve of Shadow (fire resist 1h) / Infernal Warhorn (summon 1d4 redcaps) / 100gp + +1 weapon/shield

## Intermediary Locations

### Tower of Urm (Mordenkainen)
- **Travel:** 10-16 hours
- **Hazards:** Spell-trapped halls. Glyphs of warding (lightning, confusion), animate objects, shield guardian. DC 17 Arcana to disarm.
- **Social:** Mordenkainen is a simulacrum. Impatient, experimental. DC 16 Persuasion for minor favor.
- **Loot (d4):** Amulet of Proof vs Detection / Scroll of Tasha's Otherworldly Guise / Arcane Battery (H, 3 charges, recover 3rd-level slot) / Soul Coin + +1 Wand of War Mage

### Bone Brambles (Red Ruth)
- **Travel:** 8-14 hours. DC 15 Nature/Survival halves time.
- **Hazards:** Animated skeletons (AC 13, 45 HP, multiattack), grapple traps (DC 16 Athletics, 2d6 piercing/round). Hunger of Hadar / Black Tentacles in narrow passages.
- **Social:** Red Ruth demands blood or memories. DC 18 Persuasion for reduced price.
- **Loot (d4):** Potion of Mind Reading + Superior Healing / Oracle Bone (H, advantage on Int/Wis 1/LR) / Hat of Disguise / Soul Coin + Cursed Eye Gem

### Mirror of Mephistar (Mephistopheles)
- **Travel:** 10-18 hours
- **Hazards:** 4 ice devils + bone devil lieutenant. DC 15 Acrobatics on slick surfaces.
- **Social:** Deals through Rigorath (cambion). Demands blood or 5 soul coins. DC 20 Deception for extra info.

## Blood Pay Locations

### Arkhan's Tower / Monument to Tiamat
- **Travel:** 12-20 hours
- **Goal:** Retrieve Tiamat's blood
- **Approach:** Young/wyrmling dragons attack on approach. Krull (tortle priest) arrives on adult white dragon Obatala to stop fighting.
- **Arkhan's Demand:** Sacrifice of an innocent (Lulu? unicorn from Demon Zapper? Krikendolt?)
- **Alternative:** Fight Arkhan (CR 16, 221 HP) + Torogar + Krull + manticore. Remove abishai to even odds.
- **DC 18 Persuasion** for alternate sacrifice. **DC 20 Stealth** to steal.
- **Reward:** Vial of Tiamat's blood

### Uldrak's Grave
- **Travel:** 12-20 hours
- **Goal:** Empyrean blood
- **Encounter:** Empyrean trapped as spined devil. DC 16 Persuasion. Needs dragon slain to restore form.
- **Complication:** Third party hunting the empyrean (warlords, glabrezu + tanarukks)
- **Reward:** Mote of Heavenly Light (H) — 2d6 radiant + advantage on Wis saves

### Obelisk (Ubbalux)
- **Travel:** 14-22 hours
- **Goal:** Balor blood. Balor imprisoned; someone must take its place.
- **Solution:** Convince Krikendolt (at Spawning Trees) to swap places. DC 14 Persuasion.
- **Complication:** Freed Ubbalux (balor with 4 exhaustion levels) may attack instead of cooperating.
- **DC 18 Arcana** for ritual. **DC 16 Deception** to trick Ubbalux.

### Shummrath's Pit
- **Travel:** 14-24 hours
- **Goal:** Pit fiend ichor. Dam River Styx with shattersticks, wait 24h for Shummrath to reform.
- **Requires:** Shattersticks from Wandering Emporium (salamander artificer)
- **DC 15 Strength** for shattersticks placement. **DC 17 Con** vs fumes.
- **Complication:** Weakened pit fiend (4 exhaustion) may fight when sentient.
- **Reward:** Hellfire Flail (H) — +1, 2d6 fire, 1/day fireball (4th level) if evil

## Fallen Three Locations

### Crypt of the Hellriders (Olanthius)
- **Travel:** 8-12 hours
- **Encounters:** Upscaled undead (2 deathlock masterminds + 10 zombies each). Wights and ghosts.
- **Social:** Olanthius can explain Zariel's motives. Directs to Bel.
- **Task:** Free Kostchtchie (Maw) to distract Zariel
- **Loot (d6):** Hellrider's Medal (adv vs fear 24h) / Scroll revivify or remove curse / Death Knight's Greaves (H, +2 AC, necrotic immune, -10 HP max) / Soul Coin + 500gp gems

### Haruman's Hill
- **Encounters:** Hellwasp Nest nearby optional. Haruman + narzugon forces.
- **Haruman fights to the death.** Reverts to human paladin corpse.
- **No soul remains** — must find sibriex to extract memories.
- **Jander Sunstar** can hint at sibriex location.
- **Iron trees** still hold screaming souls. DC 15 Religion to commune.

### Bel's Forge
- **Test:** 4 fire giants. Bel watches to judge worthiness.
- **Task:** Retrieve adamantine rods from Wrecked Flying Fortress.
- **Social:** Bel hates Zariel, supports the party. Telepathic deal.
- **Can also suggest:** Seek sibriex or Arches of Ulloch for rod location.

## Key Endgame Locations

### Kostchtchie's Maw
- Party knows this exists. Freeing the demon lord distracts Zariel.
- Olanthius may require this before revealing info.

### Arches of Ulloch
- **Guardian:** Ulloch the Sphinx (androsphinx). Nine Hells quiz challenge.
- Each wrong answer: Ulloch uses Roar ability. 3 wrong = fight the sphinx.
- **Nine Layers challenge:** Party teleported to each layer, must identify it while fighting/surviving hazards.
- DC 15 Int (Arcana/History/Religion) per layer. Gargauth can help (at a price).
- **The only way to reach the Bleeding Citadel** (Asmodeus hid it with powerful magic).

### Wrecked Flying Fortress
- Mini dungeon crawl for Bel's adamantine rods.
- **Firestorm:** Group DC 20 Survival. Dispel magic or fly to avoid.
- **6 vrocks** outside. Interior: bone whelks, necrotic remorhaz (CR 11, 195 HP).
- **Safe combination:** 6-6-6. Riddle: "Three numbers the same, but double in sum..."
- **Alarm:** 3 failed attempts awakens remorhaz.
- **Warlord arrival** after looting (Princeps Kovic wants the rods).
