Signature Combos

1. Darkness Bubble (the build's win-condition)
Cast Darkness on a coin (or via Eyes of the Dark for 2 SP). Drop coin inside Sickening Radiance. Enemies inside: 4d6 radiant per round + exhaustion stack + disadvantage on attacks. You see through Darkness. They don't.
2. Hound + Twinned Polymorph (L10+)
Bonus action Hound of Ill Omen imposes disadvantage on the boss's WIS save → Twinned Polymorph lands on two bosses → both are turtles. Action economy ruined for the encounter.
3. Subtle Counterspell
No verbal, no somatic, no source. Enemy caster doesn't know who's blocking them. Pair with Greater Invisibility for total anonymity.
4. Quickened Pattern + Misty Step
Drop Hypnotic Pattern on the enemy line, reposition 30 ft out of melee, all in one turn.
5. Necrotic Nova (L11+)
Necrotic Shroud (bonus) → Hound (next turn bonus, 3 SP) → Disintegrate at the marked target. Hound debuff + CHA-mod necrotic from Shroud trigger = ~75 average damage.
